Welcome to Swarm Robotics Lab:
Innovating the Future of Robotics

Swarm Robotics Lab is one of the HEC-funded labs under the National Centre for Robotics and Automation (NCRA). NCRA is a consortium of 11 labs across 13 universities in Pakistan, headquartered at NUST College of E&ME. NCRA was inaugurated at NUST College of E&ME by Prof. Ahsan Iqbal (Minister of Planning, Development, and Reforms) on May 23, 2018.

The Swarm Robotics Lab at UET Taxila has received approximately **79 Million PKR** in funding.

Join us in advancing robotics and automation in Pakistan!
